About

Ever bought food at the grocery store only to realize that you could never eat it all in a week, like you thought you could? Yeah, we have, too.

Food Fusion makes it easy for communities to share surplus or unwanted foods that would otherwise go to waste.

Over 1/3 of all food produced in the world goes uneaten. Instead of throwing your food in the trash can, just make a submission on our website and help out a fellow neighbor!

And if you're worried about spending too much money and time at the grocery store, we got you covered, too. Check out what your neighbors have donated on our website, and you may be able to receive some free food (and make new friends in the process)!
