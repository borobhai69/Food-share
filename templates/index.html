About

Ever bought food at the grocery store only to realize that you could never eat it all in a week, like you thought you could? Yeah, we have, too. In fact, over 1/3 of all food produced in the world goes uneaten.

Food Fusion is a community food-sharing app that makes it easy for everyone to donate and receive surplus or unwanted foods that would otherwise go to waste.

Instead of throwing your food in the trash can, just make a submission on our website and help out a local neighbor or food pantry!

Food pantries are welcome to use our website to receive and distribute food donations at their discretion. If you're just an individual worried about spending too much money and time at the grocery store, we got you covered, too. Check out what your neighbors have donated on our website, and you may be able to receive some free food (and make new friends in the process)!

How It Works

Option 1: Donate

Got any extra fruits, vegetables, protein, dairy, or grains at your home? Add donations to our database, and we will help you pass your food along to a neighbor or food pantry in your city!

Donate

Option 2: Receive

Whether you're a food pantry or an individual, browse the food donations in your city, and find the exact food that you are looking for! You will be able to specify the type and quantity of food that you need.
